C++ Text Adventure

For my second project I wanted to work with a personal favorite language and try my hand with classic text adventure games. Some notes:

  • - I moved while I was doing this assignment on top of which I was also unpacking from moving everything out from my dorm room. Time was rather limited.
  • I was basing alot of function on Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition, only to realize with such limited time many of the more complex mechanics I wanted to include and had to reapproach the idea
  • The block mechanic works 1/2 of the time it seems and I haven't been able to determine why it is aflip of the coin on that mechanic.
  • I definately will be doing this more in my free time. I enjoyed trying to map everything out and it would be neat to experience the careful electronic planning for DandD 5e mechanics
